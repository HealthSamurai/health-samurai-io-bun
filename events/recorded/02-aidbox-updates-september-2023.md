# Aidbox Product Updates | September 2023

This webinar showcases the latest Aidbox platform enhancements for tech leaders, software architects, healthcare IT engineers, and digital health professionals.

## Topics Covered

- **Aidbox FHIR Validator** – simplified resource validation
- **Production-Ready R4B and R5 Topic-Based Subscriptions** – with GCP Pub/Sub streaming
- **Open Source Telehealth App Template** – ready-to-use telehealth starter
- **OpenTelemetry Exporter** – performance monitoring and optimization
- **Audit Log UI** – easy access to audit events
- **Organization-Based Access Control** – with Bundle Batch Operations
- **Aidbox Forms Improvements** – autocomplete and UI-builder in pre-alpha
