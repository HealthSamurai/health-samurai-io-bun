# SMART on FHIR

This community meetup explored real-world applications of SMART on FHIR technology in healthcare interoperability.

## Speakers

- **Gasper Andrejc** – Health IT Domain Lead at Parsek
  - Topic: "SMART on FHIR out in the wild" – multiple concurrent integrations with major EMR systems and practical challenges

- **Vlad Ganshin** – Software Engineer at Health Samurai
  - Topic: Security policy implementation

- **Brendan Keeler** – Head of Product at Flexpa
  - Topic: Implementation variations across different FHIR servers

- **Vishwasrao Salunkhe** – Senior Product Software Engineer at Wolters Kluwer
  - Topic: Technical demonstration of the complete SMART on FHIR authorization workflow

- **Nikolai Ryzhikov** – CTO at Health Samurai (Host/Moderator)
  - Roundtable discussion on adoption challenges and solutions
