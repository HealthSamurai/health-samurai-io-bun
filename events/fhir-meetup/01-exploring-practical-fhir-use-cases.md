# Exploring Practical FHIR Use Cases

This webinar brought together industry experts to discuss practical applications of the FHIR standard. Topics covered included oncology data management on FHIR, Google Cloud's healthcare data platform capabilities, how UCSF leverages FHIR for innovation, and CMS Interoperability and Patient Access rules for health plans. The session addressed real-world challenges and solutions for organizations implementing FHIR standards.

## Speakers

- **Kaushal Parekh** – Distinguished Architect at Roche
- **Kalyan Pamarthy** – Generative Search and Agents for the Enterprise, Google Cloud
- **Ed Martin** – Healthcare Technology Innovation, UCSF
- **Pavel Smirnov** – CEO at Health Samurai
