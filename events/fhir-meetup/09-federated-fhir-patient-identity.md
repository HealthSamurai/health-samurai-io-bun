# Federated FHIR: Patient Identity & Record Linkage

This webinar explores how federated FHIR systems manage patient identity and record linkage across distributed healthcare environments. The discussion addresses challenges in maintaining consistent patient records while preventing duplicates and improving data quality.

## Key Topics

- Linking versus merging patient data
- Understanding federated FHIR architecture
- Implementing real-world solutions for seamless data integration
