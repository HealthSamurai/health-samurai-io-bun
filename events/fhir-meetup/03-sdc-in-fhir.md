# SDC in FHIR

This meetup explores Structured Data Capture (SDC) in FHIR, focusing on creating dynamic questionnaires and automating data capture processes. Topics include the SDC specification overview, UI form customization strategies, open-source questionnaire tools, rapid FHIR application development, and questionnaire-building solutions.

## Speakers

- **Lloyd McKenzie** – Principal Consultant, LM&A Consulting
- **Ilya Beda** – HL7 FHIR Expert
- **Paul Lynch** – Staff Scientist, National Library of Medicine
- **Scot Brown** – Senior Software Developer, UHN Digital
- **Stoyan Halkaliev** – CEO, NursIT Institute GmbH
