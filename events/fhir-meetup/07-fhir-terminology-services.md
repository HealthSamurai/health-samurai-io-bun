# FHIR Terminology Services

This webinar explores the essentials of FHIR terminology services, examining their capabilities, practical applications, and common pitfalls. The session addresses key implementation challenges and best practices for managing code systems, value sets, and concept mappings in healthcare applications.

## Speakers

- **Grahame Grieve** – FHIR Product Director at HL7
  - Topic: "Lord of the Terms" – why terminology infrastructure investments are essential for effective data exchange

- **Dr. Michael Lawley** – Lead of Health Informatics Group at Australian e-Health Research Centre, Lead Developer for Ontoserver
  - Topic: "FHIR Terminology Services with Ontoserver" – deployment architecture and technical implementation challenges

- **Nikolai Ryzhikov** – CTO at Health Samurai
  - Topic: "Practical Approach to FHIR Terminology Services" – Aidbox's Concept resource and practical terminology management strategies
