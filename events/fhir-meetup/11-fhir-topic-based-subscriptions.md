# FHIR Topic-based Subscriptions

This session explored how FHIR topic-based subscriptions are a robust way to keep various systems in sync with relevant data changes. The meetup addressed real-world challenges in healthcare data sharing and workflow optimization through subscription-based architectures.

## Speakers

- **Gino Canessa** – Principal Software Engineer at Microsoft
  - Covered subscription fundamentals, practical usage through demonstrations, and a care team notifications case study

- **Maxim Putintsev** – Software Engineer at Health Samurai
  - Provided implementation examples showing how effortlessly you can subscribe and stay informed within your FHIR server

- **Nikolai Ryzhikov** – CTO at Health Samurai (Host/Moderator)
  - Facilitated Q&A discussions
