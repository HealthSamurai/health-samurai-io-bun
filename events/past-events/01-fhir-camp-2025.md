FHIR Camp is a unique opportunity to learn directly from the creators and maintainers of FHIR. Whether you're new to FHIR or an experienced implementer, you'll find valuable sessions, hands-on workshops, and networking opportunities.

## What to Expect

- **Hands-on Workshops**: Build real FHIR applications with expert guidance
- **Deep-dive Sessions**: Learn advanced topics from FHIR specification authors
- **Networking**: Connect with fellow implementers from around the world
- **Beautiful Location**: Enjoy the Portuguese coast between sessions

## Who Should Attend

- Healthcare IT developers and architects
- Clinical informaticists
- Standards and interoperability specialists
- Product managers in healthcare technology
- Anyone working with or interested in FHIR

## Getting There

The venue is easily accessible from Lisbon Portela Airport (LIS). Take the train from Lisbon to Estoril (approximately 30 minutes) or arrange airport transfer through the hotel.
